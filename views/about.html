About Us

Food For Gut is a digital companion for people living with IBS symptoms. It was designed and developed by an IBS patient for other patients.

Your digital companion will help you discover your personal triggers and provide you insights about your health with tailorized bite-sized information so you can adjust your lifestyle in a sustainable way that fits your needs.

Food For Gut is a brainchild of Jeanet Lin, an IBS patient herself who has lived with IBS symptoms since 2013 but only managed to get a diagnosis in 2005. Several years of painful and frustration of not understanding what it is and how to deal led her to take this case in her own matters. Through years of research and personal experiences over the last three years, she's been able to design a way to recover more than 95% of her symptoms without any medication or supplement.
