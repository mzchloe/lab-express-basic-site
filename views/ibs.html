What is IBS?

Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) is a disorder in which abdominal pain is associated with a range of symptoms. Typical symptoms starts with bloating, flatulence, and abdominal pain accompanied by diarrhea, constipation, or alternating episodes of both. Other symptoms may also be present.

IBS affects 15% of the world's population and studies have shown 2/3 of them are women.
